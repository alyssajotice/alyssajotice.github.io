ABOUT ME

My name is Alyssa Jo, and I am a computer science student at Penn State! I am originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, but live in State College during the school year. I am working towards a Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science with a minor in Music Technology. I am very passionate about both programming and music, and I love doing things that combine the two. I am studying at Penn State's Schreyer Honors College, and my projected graduation is in May 2020. Through the honors college, I take intensive honors classes and will complete a research thesis during my senior year. After graduation, I plan on doing software development in the technology industry. While I love programming, I am also involved in a lot of clubs and organizations around campus. Through these organizations, I have had the opportunity to give back to my community and meet other passionate people. When I'm not studying or doing things around campus, I like to go horseback riding, practice yoga, and read and write. This past year, I wrote a blog for my rhetoric class about females in the field of engineering, which you can check out here!

Springfield FTK

I am involved in Springfield FTK, which is a special interest THON organization. The purpose of Springfield is to raise money for the Penn State Dance Marathon, which raises money for the fight against pediatric cancer. Through the organization, I have participated in fundraising efforts for THON such as canning and canvassing.
