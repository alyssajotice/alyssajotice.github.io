ABOUT ME

My name is Alyssa Jo, and I am a graduate of Penn State! I am originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, but am relocating to Seattle for my job with Microsoft. I have my Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science with honors. I was selected as the student marshal for my class, which is awarded to the top computer science student in the graduating class. Through the honors college, I took intensive honors classes and completed a research thesis, titled "Datalog Based Superset Disassembly of ELF Binaries". While I love programming, I was involved in clubs and organizations around campus when I was in college. Through these organizations, I have had the opportunity to give back to my community and meet other passionate people. When I'm not coding, I enjoy horseback riding, practicing yoga, reading, and writing. I love to travel, and spent a semester semester abroad in Prague, Czech Republic!!
